# 3-by-3-macropad
For the Emerald Makerspace Club, this is a small 9 switch + 1 rotary encoder macropad meant help students learn the basics of PCB design and mechanical keyboards.

## Components/Parts
- 1 RP2040 Pro Micro
- 9 Kalih hotswap sockets
- 9 MX switches
- 9 Cherry Keycaps
- 10 tht DO-35 diodes
- 1 Rotary Encoder
  These components were chosen to minimize cost, keep the macropad relatively basic, and to make it easy to solder.

## Firmware
The firmware is made with QMK.
